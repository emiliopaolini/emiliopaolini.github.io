--- title: "Sentiment Analysis: comparing stock prices and sentiments in tweets" excerpt: "An application, written in Java, that allows to compare Apple stock price and sentiments contained in tweets related to the Apple topic." collection: portfolio --- The aim of this project is to understand if it is possible to predict the stock price using the public opinions. In particular, I have examined the case of Apple stock price and using the tweets related to the Apple topic, I have tried to realize if there exists a correlation between the sentiment contained in the tweets and the trend in the stock market.The tweets were fetched using particular criteria (set of keywords contained in the text of the tweets).Then they have been processed using textual elaboration techniques and finally a classifier was obtained to extract the sentiment contained in the tweets.
